Payroll Cards

In this case TitanCards concludes a contract with the company holding the Funding Account. The company can then order cards for its employees, partners or members through its own Business Account platform. The cards, to which wages, commissions or other cardholder benefits of the can be transferred, can also be loaded using the Business Account platform. The cards can be loaded one by one or using a batch file by uploading all the data at once. TitanCards’ Payroll Programme enables the company to issue branded cards featuring its own image.
